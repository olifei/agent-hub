# Creative Agent

## Overview

The Creative Agent automatically generates marketing content based on trend insights and audience analysis. It produces a variety of content formats including ad copy, product descriptions, social media posts, and visual creative concepts — all aligned with brand guidelines and optimized for target audience engagement.

## Key Features

- **Multi-format Content Generation**: Creates ad copy, social posts, email campaigns, product descriptions, and landing page content
- **Brand Voice Consistency**: Maintains consistent tone, style, and messaging across all generated content
- **Audience-tailored Messaging**: Adapts language, references, and call-to-actions based on target demographic
- **A/B Variant Generation**: Produces multiple content variations for testing and optimization
- **Visual Direction**: Generates creative briefs and image prompts for visual content production

## How It Works

```
Trend Insights + Video Analysis → Content Strategy → Draft Generation → Multi-format Output
         (from Trendspotting          │                                        │
          & YouTuBuddy)        Brand Guidelines                      → To SmartCat
                               Audience Profile                        for review
```

### Input
- Trend insights (from Trendspotting Agent)
- Video content analysis (from YouTuBuddy Agent)
- Brand guidelines and tone of voice
- Target audience profile
- Campaign objectives

### Output
- Social media posts (multiple platforms)
- Ad copy (search, display, video)
- Product descriptions
- Email marketing content
- Visual creative briefs
- Hashtag and keyword sets

## Content Formats

| Format | Platforms | Variants |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Short-form copy | Instagram, TikTok, Twitter | 3-5 per brief |
| Long-form content | Blog, LinkedIn, Email | 1-2 per brief |
| Ad copy | Google Ads, Meta Ads | 5-10 per campaign |
| Product descriptions | E-commerce, Catalog | 1 per SKU |
| Video scripts | YouTube, TikTok | 2-3 per concept |

## Integration

Creative Agent receives insights from **Trendspotting** and **YouTuBuddy**, and sends generated content to **SmartCat** for compliance review:

```
Trendspotting → YouTuBuddy → Creative → SmartCat → Publish
                                ↑            │
                                └── Revise ──┘
```

If SmartCat flags content issues, Creative Agent automatically revises and resubmits.
